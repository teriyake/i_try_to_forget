i try to forget

This extension is an artistic exploration of duration, memory, and materiality in the context of digital interaction.

How It Works

To create "memories," the extension will occasionally and randomly save small, harmless snippets of text and images from the websites you visit. These fragments will then be displayed on other pages you browse.

Your Privacy is Important

  • All collected data is stored only on your local computer.
  • This data is never sent to any server or shared with anyone.
  • You can view, manage, and delete all your data at any time from the extension's popup icon.
